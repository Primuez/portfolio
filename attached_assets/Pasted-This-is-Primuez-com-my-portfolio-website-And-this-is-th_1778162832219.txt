🔥 Iss Live Session Mein Kya Milega?
Yeh session website design ki nahi hai. Yeh hai —
“AI Agency website ke liye aisa content kaise likhein jo client ko premium price dene pe convince kare.”
✅ Hero Section ki exact copy jo 3 second mein visitor ko hook kare
✅ Services Page kaise likho jo ₹50K–₹2L wale projects laaye
✅ About Section mein trust kaise build karo (chahe aap solo freelancer ho)
✅ Case Studies & Portfolio kaise present karo — even if aapke paas sirf 1-2 projects hain
✅ CTAs jo actually clicks aur qualified leads laate hain
✅ Pricing Page Psychology — kaise dikhao ki aap “sasta wala freelancer” nahi ho
✅ FAQ Section jo client ke 90% objections pehle hi solve kar de
Aur sabse zaroori — 🤖 AI ka use karke yeh saara content 1-2 hours mein kaise generate karein, agar aap writer nahi ho tab bhi!
